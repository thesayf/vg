About Us

Ready when you need it moved now

MoversPro is the first app-based platform in the UK to provide on-demand goods transportation. We connect customers with the nearest professional, fully insured drivers to them. We aim to be the most efficient logistics provider globally, matching customers with drivers in the shortest times, and giving full transparency for the customers to track the status of their move live.

So why did we build it?

We started MoversPro after seeing the frustration and trouble customers were having in getting reliable and cheap, last minute logistics solutions. We saw the moving market in need of disruption and have worked hard over the past 5 years developing the best solution to give you the cheapest prices, and the quickest, most reliable and trustworthy service that you can track fully online.
